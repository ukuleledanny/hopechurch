Our Story

A Church Built on Hope

Hope Church began with a simple conviction: that the gospel of Jesus Christ changes everything. We planted our roots in Madison, Alabama because we believe every community deserves a church that is committed to the long, faithful work of discipleship.

We are not a perfect church. We are a gathering of people who know they need grace, and who want to extend that same grace to everyone around them. Sunday mornings at Hope are a reflection of that — unhurried, genuine, and anchored in God's Word.

We hold weekly services at 10:00 a.m. and gather in community groups throughout the week. Whatever stage of life you're in, we hope you'll find a home here.

Doctrine

What We Believe

We are a confessional, evangelical church. Below are the core convictions that anchor everything we do.

Scripture

We believe the Bible is the inspired, inerrant Word of God — the final authority for all faith and practice.

God

We believe in one God, eternally existing in three persons: Father, Son, and Holy Spirit.

Jesus Christ

We believe Jesus is fully God and fully man — born of a virgin, crucified for our sins, risen bodily, and coming again.

Salvation

We believe salvation is by grace alone, through faith alone, in Christ alone — and that it transforms every area of life.

The Church

We believe the local church is God's primary instrument for making disciples, caring for souls, and advancing the gospel.

Eternity

We believe in the bodily resurrection of the dead, the final judgment, and the eternal glory of all who trust in Christ.
